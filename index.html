ABOUT US

Welcome to La Fabrique Marseillaise, the first wellness center where the sources don’t flow with water but with pure pastis, straight from the heart of Marseille’s calanques!

Legend tells that nestled in the hills of Marseille lies a secret source, discovered by a local beekeeper known as Marius Le Merveilleux. While perfecting a new honey recipe, Marius stumbled upon this extraordinary source full of an elixir rich with the flavors of anise and sunshine, affectionately called "Marseille’s liquid gold." Since then, this magical source has attracted visitors from around the globe, eager to experience our signature treatment: la Pastisothérapie.

At this unique wellness center, blending laughter and relaxation, you’ll leave renewed: with a golden tan, a carefree spirit, and, most importantly, 100% Marseillais. Whether you’re a Parisian looking for an escape or a serious Lyonnais in need of some sun, La Fabrique Marseillaise is the ultimate destination to recharge, basking in the warmth of pastis and the Mediterranean sun!
